What is Games?

We're not your average game studio. We're the rebellious offspring of classic social games like charades, the ones who ditched the stuffy rules and built a laughter empire. Think of us as the cool cousin who skipped textbooks in favor of stand-up comedy workshops.

Heard whispers of laughter echoing from our games through articles, streams, commercials, or social media? Or maybe a friend spilled the beans about our legendary parties? Well, consider yourself officially invited!

What is a Party?

Since 2016, we haven't stopped churning out the hilarity! Every year, we unleash a brand new Party, each one jam-packed with 5 unique games built for 1-8 players. Got a bigger crew?

No sweat! We know popularity can be a burden (wink wink), that's why many of our games let the audience play along.

So even with a massive party, everyone gets to be part of the laughter riot.

Is there anything my friends or family need to do to play with me?

Here's the beauty of Party: your friends can join the hilarity without spending a dime! Forget app stores and in-game purchases – we're all about keeping things simple.

How do I get a Game Started?

Alright party animal, you've got the Party, your crew is assembled, and the good times are practically pulsating in the air! The best part? You don't need to decipher a dusty rulebook to get started.

For your party crew (both the in-person and virtual attendees) to join the laughter riot, there's one key ingredient: seeing the game screen! Think of it like a comedy club – you gotta see the funny bits to crack up.
